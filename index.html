About Me

I am a student of the Department of Information Technology at the State Polytechnic of Malang. I like to learn about information technology, especially in web programming and problem solving. In implementing my learning, I also made several web applications to complete assignments from lectures or wanted to create a web app.

Junior Web Developer

in August 2021 I passed the Junior Web Developer certification from the Indonesian professional certification authority after I attended the Junior Web Developer training organized by BPPTIK (Balai Pelatihan dan Pengembangan Teknologi Informasi dan Komunikasi), Kemenkominfo RI.

Junior Graphic Designer

in October 2021 I passed the Junior Graphic Designer certification from the Indonesian professional certification authority after I attended the Junior Graphic Designer training organized by the Digital Talent Scholarship, Kemenkominfo RI.

Certifed Public Speaking Proffesional

in November 2021 I received the non-academic CERTIFIED PUBLIC SPEAKING PROFESSIONAL (CPSP) degree from the IEEEL INSTITUTE after I attended the CERTIFIED PUBLIC SPEAKING PROFESSIONAL training organized by Welldone Skill.

Organizational Experience
